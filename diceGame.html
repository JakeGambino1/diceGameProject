Rules for the game:

Weapon type advantage grants +1 damage bonus in a rock/paper/scissors triangle.
Sword > Hammer > Shield > Sword

Every attack has a 1 in 8 chance to miss. Rolling an 8 means you missed.

Stagger is a mechanic applied on every attack. If you stagger, you suffer a -1 penalty to your defense rating for the duration of the game.
Stagger is calculated by rolling a 6-sided dice twice. The first roll determines the base.
